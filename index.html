About Me

I'm a junior majoring in physics and computer science at Amherst College and a visiting research student at the University of Oxford. I fell in love with programming, while encountering statistical mechanics in high school. I'm a voracious learner of new technologies, hands always itching to build things. Besides being a developer, I'm a core committee member of ACM-ICPC club and Public Relations Officer of Artificial Intelligence Club at Oxford/Amherst. Oh, and I'm a competitive Dota 2 player!
